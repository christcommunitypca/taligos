Privacy

Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Taligos Apps website, support requests, and Taligos apps, including OneUp, TallyUp, NameDrop, WordDrop, and ReceiptDrop.

Effective date: April 2, 2026

What we collect

We may collect the information you choose to send us, such as your name, email address, app name, device details, app version, support message, screenshots, and other information you include in a support request or contact message.

How we use it

We use that information to respond to your questions, troubleshoot bugs, improve our apps and website, protect our services, and keep basic records of support requests.

What we do not do

Taligos Apps does not sell your personal information. We do not rent your personal information. We do not use your support messages for advertising.

When we may share information

We may share information only when needed to run the website or support process, comply with the law, protect users, or protect Taligos Apps. If we use service providers, they may process information only for those limited purposes.

Retention and security

We keep information for as long as reasonably needed to provide support, improve the apps, meet legal obligations, or protect our services. No internet system is perfectly secure, but we use reasonable steps to protect the information we receive.

Your choices

You can contact hello@taligos.com to ask what support information we have from you, to request corrections, or to request deletion, subject to legal and operational needs.

Children's privacy

Taligos Apps is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through this website or our support forms.

Third-party links and stores

Apps and website pages may link to the App Store or other third-party services. Those services have their own terms and privacy practices.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and change the effective date above.
